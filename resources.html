If your goal is to self teach yourself japanese, there are a number of tools you can use to achieve this goal. This are all the tools that I use:

  • Book: Minna No Nihongo. It comes with a vocabulary book, and an audio cd. Amazon link here!
  • Youtube playlists: Complementary to Minna No Nihongo. Vocabulary and grammar playlists.
  • Mobile apps:
    • Kana Town (for practicing Katakana and Hiragana, Android version).
    • Obenkyo (for Hiragana and Katakana stroke orders, Android version).
    • LingoDeer, to complement Minna No Nihongo.
